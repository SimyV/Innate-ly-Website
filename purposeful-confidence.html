How do we build purposeful confidence?

Many strategies are available…

The importance of purpose is well understood and studied. As a result, there are many approaches for how to go about “finding your purpose.” From Forbes articles to Harvard Business Review commentary, – from positive psychology’s VIA Character Institute to Simon Sinek’s “Start with Why” – we are surrounded by commentary about the role purpose plays in our lives and various paths to work out our purpose.

The two most important days in your life are the day you are born and the day you find out why.
—Mark Twain

The trouble is, despite the availability of many self-help guides, most people struggle to articulate their purpose, let alone know how to live it.

Often, this search for purpose leads to its own form of purpose-finding anxiety.

…Some strategies are better than others

Purpose, meaning and belonging are all outcomes. When we are able to live our lives and contribute in a way that is meaningful to us, we experience purpose. Therefore, the question is, “How am I able to uniquely contribute?” or put differently, “What is my core gift I am meant to give the world?” Innate-ly supports its clients to understand their gifts, articulate their core gift, and discover how to give their core gift.

"Deep in our bones lies an intuition that we arrive here carrying a bundle of gifts to offer to the community. Over time, these gifts are meant to be seen, developed, and called into the [Global] village at times of need.”
– Francis Weller

What is the philosophy of gifts?

Based on the work of Bruce Anderson, founder of the Core Gift Institute, gift philosophy is founded in ancient wisdom. For forty years, Bruce has been researching and building systems to support individuals in finding inspiration and meaning in life as well as motivating members of organizations to combine their gifts to add value and build an engaging workplace. When you are clear on your core gift in life, you are able to act with purposeful confidence, walk a path that is meaningful, and live a life focused on honing your ability to give your gift.

  • Create Meaning

    Define your core gift and see the path you’ve been on your whole life. Through the lens of your core gift, your successes and failures, your wins and losses – they are all connected to your core gift.

  • Inform Decisions

    Are you a people-leader by title or by influence? Connecting with your core gift creates an authenticity and confidence. Giving your gift and appreciating the gifts of others will leave a lasting impact on those around you.

What is a Core Gift Discovery Interview?

A core gift discovery interview can help you immediately identify your core gift. In a two-hour journey, you'll answer questions that guide you on your own journey and create a personalized core gift statement.

What is gifts-based coaching?

Whether you're a seasoned executive seeking to amplify your leadership impact or an aspiring leader looking for ways to develop yourself, a team of highly experienced and certified coaches can help you create the conditions in your life and organisation that help you improve how you guide, connect, and empower yourself - and others.

How to approach gifts-based team development?

  • Completion of your Core Gift Discovery Interview (1 session)

  • Integration of your gift with your organisations’ mission and values

  • Exploration of how you are using your gift today to connect and empower those you lead

  • Articulation of your actions to expand the giving of your unique gift
