How do we build purposeful confidence?

Many strategies are available…

The importance of purpose is well understood and studied. As a result, there are many approaches for how to go about “finding your purpose.” From Forbes articles to Harvard Business Review commentary, – from positive psychology’s VIA Character Institute to Simon Sinek’s “Start with Why” – we are surrounded by commentary about the role purpose plays in our lives and various paths to work out our purpose.

The two most important days in your life are the day you are born and the day you find out why. —Mark Twain

The trouble is, despite the availability of many self-help guides, most people struggle to articulate their purpose, let alone know how to live it. Often, this search for purpose leads to its own form of purpose-finding anxiety.

…Some strategies are better than others

Purpose, meaning and belonging are all outcomes. When we are able to live our lives and contribute in a way that is meaningful to us, we experience purpose. Therefore, the question is, “How am I able to uniquely contribute?” or put differently, “What is my core gift I am meant to give the world?” Innate-ly supports its clients to understand their gifts, articulate their core gift, and discover how to give their core gift.

"Deep in our bones lies an intuition that we arrive here carrying a bundle of gifts to offer to the community. Over time, these gifts are meant to be seen, developed, and called into the [Global] village at times of need.” – Francis Weller

What is the philosophy of gifts?

Based on the pioneering work of Bruce Anderson, founder of the Core Gift Institute, gift philosophy is founded in ancient wisdom. Bruce has invested 40 years researching and building systems and processes based on his findings fromworking with wide and diverse members of society. From supporting individuals living on the streets to find inspiration and meaning in life to motivating members of organisations to combine their gifts to add value and build an engaging workplace, the gifts philosophy give us our unique path to follow in our own life. We can all learn to appreciate the philosophy of gifts by exploring and valuing the diversity of our past experiences and how they come to shape what we bring to our interactions today. Every person is born with a core gift, something innately built into who we are, strengthened over our lives through our experiences and challenges. The notion of gifts is consistent with the modern study of positive psychology and strengths, at the same time, it goes one step further in helping individuals to articulate their core gift which (unlike strengths) is unique to each of us. Whether your community is a family, a village, a workplace, a city, a state, a nation (or all of them combined) when you are clear on your core gift in life, you are able to act with purposeful confidence, walk a path that is meaningful, and live a life focused on honing your ability to give your gift.

What does a gifts workshop help me to do?

  • Create Meaning

    Define your core gift and see the path you’ve been on your whole life. Through the lens of your core gift, your successes and failures, your wins and losses – they are all connected to your core gift.

  • Inform Decisions

    Decisions in life are difficult when made without a guiding framework or light. Your core gift can help you to see which decision is truest to your path and to confidently pursue it.

  • Navigate life transitions

    Career changes, relocations, raising a family, or just generally feeling lost and needing a transition but not knowing how… transitions are a time of one thing coming to an end and another just beginning. Your core gift can help you navigate this shift and enter into the new phase with clarity.

  • Amplify your positive impact on the world

    Are you a people-leader by title or by influence? Connecting with your core gift creates an authenticity and confidence that is both affirmative and humble. Giving your gift and learning to appreciate the gifts of others will leave a lasting impact on those you lead and work with every day.

What is a Core Gift Discovery Interview?

A core gift discovery interview is the only known process that can help you to immediately identify your core gift. In this unique two hour process, you will be guided on your own journey, answering a variety of questions. These questions are crafted and your answers collated in a way to help you see the most important answers which lead to an individualised Core Gift Statement. There are no predefined answers or choices to your questions… only you and your life experiences to inform this personalised process.

What is gifts-based coaching?

Whether you’re a seasoned executive seeking to amplify your leadership impact or an aspiring leader looking for compelling ways to develop yourself, coaching is a powerful approach to help you create the conditions in your life and organisation to help you improve how you guide, connect, and empower yourself - and others. With a team of highly experienced and certified coaches, Innate-ly can help you to articulate your core gift and integrate it into your organisation(s) and your life. A typical gift-based coaching program is 3-5 coaching sessions.

How to approach gifts-based team development?

  • Completion of your Core Gift Discovery Interview (1 session)
  • Integration of your gift with your organisations’ mission and values
  • Exploration of how you are using your gift today to connect and empower those you lead
  • Articulation of your actions to expand the giving of your unique gift
