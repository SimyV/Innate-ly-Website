How do we create more meaningful connections?

Many strategies are available…

There is no shortage of quick tips available for you to increase the possibility of connecting socially with others:

  • Signing up for common interest groups.
  • Physically embracing loved ones more often.
  • Volunteering in local community organisations

These are valuable and worthwhile endeavours. However, it is helpful to realise that not all interactions with others are created equally.

…Some strategies are better than others

Sometimes the way we interact with others and the stories we tell ourselves about those interactions may decrease our connection. Most of our interaction is governed by the way we communicate both with the world around us and internally with ourselves. Innate-ly supports its clients to upgrade their communication approaches and see the unique gifts of those around us, thereby improving and increasing the connection in our lives.

  • Physical proximity to loved ones
  • The sense of belonging to a group
  • Support provided by friends, family, colleagues and more.

What is compassionate communication?

Based on the work of clinical psychologist, Marshall Rosenberg and his research around Non-Violent Communication (NVC), we support clients to explore how common approaches to communicating with others can unintentionally damage relationships and instead build a practice of communicating based on self-compassion and empathy. The use of Compassionate Communication in your life will…

How can I communicate with compassion?

  • Create Connection can allow you to create more meaningful connection by building awareness of your own personal needs and with curiosity of needs for those with whom you’re communicating.
  • Dissolve Conflict Training can dissolve conflict in your relationships by building a greater sense of empathy for others and finding strategies to move forward together
  • Building Resilience through emotional exploration, a greater discernment of where you are directing your energy, and ownership for how you are interacting with the world around you.
  • Transform Relationships by embedding increased support, kindness, and courage to explore even the most difficult conversations
