Accessibility Statement

Innate-ly Pty Ltd, is committed to providing an inclusive and accessible experience to everyone, including those with disabilities. It is Innate-ly Pty Ltd’s policy to ensure that persons with disabilities have full and equal opportunity to access and benefit from the goods, services, privileges, advantages, and accommodations offered by Innate-ly Pty Ltd, through Innate-ly.com and the PQ mobile app. To ensure meaningful access and provide effective communication to persons with disabilities, Innate-ly Pty Ltd, has undertaken the following measures:

Notice

Innate-ly Pty Ltd, provides this policy statement and notice, prominently and directly linked from the Innate-ly.com homepage, soliciting feedback from visitors to Innate-ly.com on how accessibility can be improved. We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Innate-ly.com and the PQ mobile app. Please let us know if you encounter any barriers to access:

Email: Unlock@Innate-ly.com

Standards

Innate-ly Pty Ltd, has adopted and shall make every reasonable effort for Innate-ly.com and the PQ mobile app to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (“WCAG”) 2.1 Level AA to improve accessibility and user experience. WCAG is published by the World Wide Web Consortium (“W3C”), Web Accessibility Initiative (“WAI”), available at www.w3.org/TR/WCAG/ The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines are technical standards to improve digital accessibility.

Programming

To create an optimal experience across a variety of browser, device, and assistive technology combinations, Innate-ly.com relies upon the following languages and specifications:

• HTML

• CSS

• JavaScript

Note that different technology combinations may interpret code in unique ways and render experiences that are specific to those combinations.

Compatibility

Innate-ly.com is designed to be compatible with assistive technologies and the last two versions of major browsers including Chrome, FireFox, and Safari. Assistive technologies include NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, and TalkBack screen readers. Innate-ly.com may not display optimally in Internet Explorer 10 or older browsers.

Coordinator

Innate-ly Pty Ltd, has designated a Web Accessibility Coordinator who is knowledgeable on web accessibility, and responsible for carrying out this policy.

Distribution

Innate-ly Pty Ltd, makes available and annually distributes this policy statement to all personnel that design, develop, maintain, manage, or otherwise have responsibility for the content and format of Innate-ly.com.

Compliance

Through Innate-ly Pty Ltd’s proactive efforts and genuine commitment to accessibility and following best practices set forth by relevant laws and governing bodies, including Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) as well as other applicable anti-discrimination laws.
